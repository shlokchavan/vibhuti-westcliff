Pet adoption

The process of transferring responsibility for a pet that was previously owned by another party such as a person, shelter, or rescue organization


Adoption process

The central issue in adoption is whether the new adopter can provide a safe, secure, and permanent home for the adopted pet. Responsible shelters, pounds, and rescue organizations refuse to supply pets to people whom they deem ineligible based on assessing their inability to supply the adopted animal with a suitable home.
